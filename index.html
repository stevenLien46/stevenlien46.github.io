About Me

Hi there! I’m a Front End Developer based in Toronto with a love for clean code, well-built websites, and solving problems with a creative twist. I've worked with some great teams at places like Maropost and AutoTrader, where I've been involved in everything from full website migrations to A/B testing, cross-platform CMS builds, and collaborating across time zones with global teammates.

I'm fluent in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and have plenty of hands-on experience with WordPress, HubSpot, Salesforce, Webflow—you name it. Whether it's troubleshooting bugs, building custom templates, or turning a mockup into a pixel-perfect live page, I enjoy diving in and making things work.

Outside of coding, I'm big on movement and adventure. I’m super passionate about health and fitness—I’ve completed the Mississauga Marathon (2025), the Sporting Life 10k (2018 & 2019), and tackled the Spartan Race Super back in 2017. One of my proudest moments? Reaching Everest Base Camp in Nepal in 2019—an unforgettable experience. When I’m not running or training, I’m usually trying my hand at a new sport, because if it gets me moving, I’m in.

Whether it’s building intuitive websites or climbing mountains (literally or metaphorically), I’m always up for the next challenge. Let’s create something awesome together!
